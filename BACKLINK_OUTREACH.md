# NameBuzz — Backlink Outreach Templates

## Why These Templates Work
- Short, specific, no filler
- Personalised per contact type
- Clear value to the recipient
- Single CTA

## How to Use
1. Find contacts via LinkedIn, Twitter, or their website contact form
2. Send from info@buildspark.com.au via gog
3. Track replies in CRM
